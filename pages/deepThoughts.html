Deep Thoughts

Is water wet? I say yes. The definition of being wet is having water or some liquid on an object, and as water molecules touch other water molecules, that makes water wet.

What is consciousness? What makes something conscious? If we are conscious, are our bodies conscious? And are we alive? Our cells are alive, are their organelles alive? At what point in the size spectrum does something stop being alive? If we are alive, our organs are alive, and our cells are, are the organelles in those cells alive? If so, can we consider the proteins that make up the organelles alive? Even if you say no, remember that alive things are made of things that are not alive...
